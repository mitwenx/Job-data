SSC CGL 2026 (Combined Graduate Level Examination) has been announced by the Staff Selection Commission for recruitment to various Group B and Group C posts in Ministries, Departments and Organizations of the Government of India.

Posts include Tax Assistant, Auditor, Sub Inspector, Inspector, Assistant Section Officer and more across central government offices.

Selection Process:
- Tier I: Computer Based Examination (100 questions, 200 marks)
- Tier II: Computer Based Examination (Paper I mandatory, Paper II/III for specific posts)
- Document Verification
- Medical Examination

How to Apply:
1. Visit https://ssc.nic.in
2. Register with valid email and mobile number
3. Fill the application form with personal and educational details
4. Upload photograph, signature and required documents
5. Pay the examination fee online
6. Submit and take printout for future reference

Important: Candidates must have completed graduation from a recognized university on or before the closing date of application.
