Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) have published the notification for recruitment of Assistant Loco Pilots (ALP) and Technicians in Indian Railways. This is one of the largest recruitment drives for 10th pass candidates.

Eligibility:
- Minimum 10th class pass from a recognized board
- Must hold ITI certification in relevant trade OR Diploma in Engineering
- Medical fitness as per Indian Railway medical standards (visual acuity requirements for ALP)

Selection Process:
1. CBT Stage I: 75 questions, 60 minutes (Mathematics, General Intelligence, General Science, General Awareness)
2. CBT Stage II: Part A (relevant trade) + Part B (Engineering Drawing, Mathematics, General Science)
3. Computer Based Aptitude Test (for ALP post only — tests memory, concentration and following instructions)
4. Document Verification
5. Medical Examination

How to Apply:
1. Visit https://rrbapply.gov.in
2. Create account with valid email and mobile number
3. Select your preferred RRB zone
4. Fill personal, educational and other details
5. Upload photo, signature and other documents
6. Pay fee online via Debit Card, Credit Card, UPI or Net Banking
7. Submit application before last date

Note: Candidates with colour blindness are not eligible for ALP post but can apply for Technician posts.
